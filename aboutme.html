Photography:

I am a Nikon fan and you can guess what DSLR I have. You're right! I have a Nikon D7000 and it's been a great camera and friend to me. It's like a buddy that I won't forget to bring along with wherever I go.

I am mainly a landscape photographer but occasionally street photography as well. Hence, that's why I always carry my 24mm f/2.8D prime lens with me. I don't have much to say about that lens except that it is a sharp and fast lens. Overall, it is an amazing lens that I would recommend it to anyone that owns a Nikon out there!

Road Biking:

I started getting into road biking recently and I must say that it's a very enjoyable sport/recreation. I learned how to ride a bicycle since I was younger but I never knew that riding a road bike requires different riding technicques.

I ride a Fuji Roubaix 1.0 and it's a pretty comfortable ride (for a road bike!). Every road bike is different and this one has that "snappy" feeling which I like.

My favourite bike trails so far are the Manhattan Beach Trail and the Santa Ana River Trail. So if you want to catch me, you will probably find me on one of those trails!
